						Order Follow:

Cart Page:
The user reviews their cart and clicks "Proceed to Checkout."
An order is created in the database with:
Cart items
User details (if available)
An initial status, e.g., pending or draft.



Checkout Page:
The user enters their shipping, billing, and other details.
The order in the database is updated with the entered details.



Payment Page:
After completing the payment, the order status is updated to completed or paid.
